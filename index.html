School Achievements

Pi Day
Great and engaging competition and fun activities

Won 5th place in competition where we had to memorize and say the digits of pi, as many digits as we can. The activities are very fun and engaging and we can have a great time.

Talent show
Nice and fun way for kids to spend their free time

The talent show was an exciting oppertunity to do and I enjoyed it a lot. A lot of kids,including me, were singing. All of my classmates said that I had the best voice out of everyone else who sang ( -_-) I was so happy to hear that!

P.R.I.D.E. Ceremony
Great experience to win and be recognized!

Won P.R.I.D.E. Ceremony in June for letter D, which stands for Determination. It felt great to hear your name be called and hear all the great things the teacher wants to say about you. I got an encouraging award signed by the principals of my school, and my family and teachers were very proud of me!

What's New

  • New Lunch Room Seating Rule

    Because of the continuing COVID-19, schools have made a new rule saying that once students pick a spot in the lunch room to sit, they must sit in that spot during lunch for the rest of the school year. This was intended to make it easier for teachers and parents to quarintine their kids if a person in a row is tested positive for COVID-19

  • Schools Are Now In Person With No Other Options

    Unlike the 2019-2020 and 2020-2021 school year, the 2021-2022 school year is going to be fully in person with no options for remote or hybrid for most schools, even in schools where kids are not vaccinated.
